Step-by-Step Guide

1. Complete secondary school with excellent results.
2. Enroll in a recognized law school or university.
3. Earn a Bachelor of Laws (LL.B.) degree.
4. Pass the Certificate in Legal Practice (CLP) or equivalent.
5. Complete chambering/pupillage under a law firm.
6. Get called to the Bar and start practicing as a lawyer.
