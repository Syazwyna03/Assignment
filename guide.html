Steps to Achieve My Goal as a Lawyer

Complete SPM with excellent results
Enroll in a recognized pre-law or foundation program
Obtain a Bachelor's Degree in Law (LLB)
Undertake a chambering period with a legal firm
Pass the Certificate in Legal Practice (CLP) or equivalent
Get called to the Bar and become a qualified advocate & solicitor
Pursue specialization or postgraduate law studies
